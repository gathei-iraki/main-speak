Welcome to Speak Out

Where every story matters

The Speak Out project seeks to bring light to the topic of mental health which is regarded as a taboo in many African countries. Speak Out will raise awareness on the various mental issues, assist in finding available and affordable resources for those seeking help and most importantly eliminate the stigma associated with mental health.
